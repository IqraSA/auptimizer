aup.EE - Experiment Execution¶

Overview¶

aup.EE supports all experiment execution-related code.

  • aup.EE.Experiment controls the flow of an experiment.

  • aup.EE.Job takes care of job execution.

  • aup.EE.Resource provides the backbone where aup.EE.Job runs on.
